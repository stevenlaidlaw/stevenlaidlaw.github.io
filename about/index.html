About

I write novels, short stories, and screenplays. You can find my currently available works in the ‘Books’ section of this website.

I grew up in Western Australia. I moved to the big city when I was old enough to, and still with my wife, two daughters, and a pair of trouble making cats.

You can find my latest updates on this site and Twitter. Or subscribe to my mailing list in the sidebar. I don’t spam, and don;t update more than once every two months. Promise.

FAQ

When is your next book coming out? See the progress bar on the right? That’s about as close an estimate as you’re going to get I’m afraid.

Will [insert_character] [insert_action]?? You’ll have to wait for the next book to find out!

How many books set in the Pulse universe are you planning on writing? At minimum three. Ideally six, as I have a second trilogy in mind, but we will have to see how it goes as I do have a few other standalone novels and series I plan on writing before getting back around to the second trilogy.

Can I ask you a question about the series, or writing in general? You can ask, but if it’s regarding something that is yet to be revealed I won’t be able to tell you. For anything else, and any writing questions, yes feel free to contact me! The best way is probably the ‘Ask the Author’ section in the sidebar. I try to answer every email I get, but can’t promise it will be right away.
