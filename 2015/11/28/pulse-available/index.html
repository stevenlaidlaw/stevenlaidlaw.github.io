Blurb

Pickpocket Alexandra Murray has grown up in the bad part of New York City. She has spent her whole life learning that you can never rely on anyone but yourself. Since terrorist attacks gave the Military power to act on US soil, it has made life hard for someone who relies on stealing to survive.

That is until her sixteenth birthday, when life as she knows it is changed forever. Will this be the chance that she has been looking for to make a new life for herself, or will misplaced trust and bad decisions see her lose her very freedom?
